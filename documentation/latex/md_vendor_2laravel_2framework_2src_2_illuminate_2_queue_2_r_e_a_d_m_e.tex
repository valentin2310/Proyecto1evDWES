 The Laravel Queue component provides a unified API across a variety of different queue services. Queues allow you to defer the processing of a time consuming task, such as sending an e-\/mail, until a later time, thus drastically speeding up the web requests to your application.\hypertarget{md_vendor_2laravel_2framework_2src_2_illuminate_2_queue_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md915}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Usage Instructions}{Usage Instructions}}\label{md_vendor_2laravel_2framework_2src_2_illuminate_2_queue_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md915}
