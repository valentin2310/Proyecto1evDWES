Collision was created by, and is maintained by {\bfseries{\href{https://github.com/nunomaduro}{\texttt{ Nuno Maduro}}}}, and is a package designed to give you beautiful error reporting when interacting with your app through the command line.


\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item It\textquotesingle{}s included on {\bfseries{\href{https://laravel.com}{\texttt{ Laravel}}}}, the most popular free, open-\/source PHP framework in the world.
\item Built on top of the {\bfseries{\href{https://github.com/filp/whoops}{\texttt{ Whoops}}}} error handler.
\item Supports \href{https://github.com/laravel/laravel}{\texttt{ Laravel}}, \href{https://symfony.com}{\texttt{ Symfony}}, \href{https://github.com/sebastianbergmann/phpunit}{\texttt{ PHPUnit}}, and many other frameworks.
