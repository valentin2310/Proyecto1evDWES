Laravel Prompts is a PHP package for adding beautiful and user-\/friendly forms to your command-\/line applications, with browser-\/like features including placeholder text and validation.

Laravel Prompts is perfect for accepting user input in your \href{https://laravel.com/docs/artisan\#writing-commands}{\texttt{ Artisan console commands}}, but it may also be used in any command-\/line PHP project.\hypertarget{md_vendor_2laravel_2prompts_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md917}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{Official Documentation}{Official Documentation}}\label{md_vendor_2laravel_2prompts_2_r_e_a_d_m_e_autotoc_md917}
