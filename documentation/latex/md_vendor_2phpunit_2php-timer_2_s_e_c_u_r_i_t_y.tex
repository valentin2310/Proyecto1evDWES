 This library is intended to be used in development environments only. For instance, it is used by the testing framework \doxylink{namespace_p_h_p_unit}{PHPUnit}. There is no reason why this library should be installed on a webserver.

{\bfseries{If you upload this library to a webserver then your deployment process is broken. On a more general note, if your {\ttfamily vendor} directory is publicly accessible on your webserver then your deployment process is also broken.}}\hypertarget{md_vendor_2phpunit_2php-timer_2_s_e_c_u_r_i_t_y_autotoc_md1527}{}\doxysection{\texorpdfstring{Security Contact Information}{Security Contact Information}}\label{md_vendor_2phpunit_2php-timer_2_s_e_c_u_r_i_t_y_autotoc_md1527}
